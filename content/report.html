--- title: Report an incident to NCSC ---

Report an incident to NCSC

If you want to report an IT security incident to NCSC, please use the following reporting form:

  • NCSC Reporting Form

Point of contact for CERTs and CSIRTs

As GovCERT.ch is the technical team of the NCSC, the following email address can be considered as point of contact for FIRST members and other CERTs/CSIRTs: incidents[at]govcert{dot}ch. Please also direct any inquieries or reports regarding critical IT infrastructure in Switzerland to this email address. If you wish to communicate through a secure channel, please use our PGP key or S/MIME certificate.

Report a phishing site or phishing email

If you want to report a phishing site or phishing email, you can report them to antiphishing.ch or forward the email.

  • antiphishing.ch
  • reports{at}antiphishing[dot]ch

Report a crime

If you wish to report a crime, please contact your local police station.

Report extortion by email

If you are being extorted by email, please visit the following page for more information:

  • stop-sextortion.ch

General / other inquiries

For media inquiries please contact the NCSC media representatives using the following email address:

Media inquiries: ncsc-media[at]gs-efd.admin{dot}ch

If you have any other inquiries or you don't know who to contact, you can send your request to the GovCERT directly using the following email address:

General inquiries: outreach[at]govcert{dot}ch
